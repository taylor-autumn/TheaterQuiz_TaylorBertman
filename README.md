# TheaterQuiz_TaylorBertman

Hello! This is my ReadMe File for my quiz/form/tool assignment in Adv CC. 

Basically, I wanted to make a quiz that would challenge people who write off theater as something they are not interested in because they are simply wrong.
As someone deeply involved in the theater community (both in work and interest) theater is such a diverse world of art and expression, whether be through the common thread of performance period or the intricate tying of plot and music and structure that becomes musicals and plays. 
Regardless of what one may think, there is some kind of performance art they like, and if they are writing it off (which I have been arounud many many people who do this/say this so this is something I have encountered) they are sealing away a whole world of identity, expression, and meaning that can resonate with people simply because they won't give it a chance.
I hope that with this project, people are encouraged to take a look into any kind of performance because there is surely no way they won't like at least aspect of it. That's all I am saying, give it a chance! You never know when it will surprise you and now you have a whole new hyperfixation to deal with....
                                                - Taylor Bertman
